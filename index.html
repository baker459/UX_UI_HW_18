Annie Baker

UX/UI Designer

I am a creative and driven individual who has always had a keen interest in design.

I started my career in the hospitality industry where I honed my skills in customer service and problem-solving, all while constantly being inspired by the different cultures and designs I encountered in my world travels.
Despite my success in the hospitality industry, I always felt drawn towards design and decided to pursue my dream of becoming a UX/UI Designer. I enrolled in a bootcamp program offered by the University of Oregon where I was able to learn the necessary skills and techniques to turn my passion into a career.
My dedication and natural talent for design allowed me to create intuitive and user-friendly interfaces that addressed the needs of different users from project to project. As I embark on this new chapter in my career as a UX/UI Designer, I remain committed to using my creativity and skills to create designs that are not only beautiful and functional, but also compassionate and inclusive.

MY WORK

"Water U Doing?" Hydration Competition Prototype

People are not motivated to stay hydrated despite how crucial it is to our health. The solution is to offer health-conscious people a mobile application to track their hydration and compete in varying hydration challenges against themselves and/or friends.

Washington Department of Licensing Website Redesign

I had five weeks to execute the design process: research, define, ideate, prototype, and test a responsive website solution for the Washington Department of Licensing.
